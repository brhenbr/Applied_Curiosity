# ✍️ Essays

> **"Writing is nature's way of letting you know how sloppy your thinking is."** — *Guindon*

## About this Collection
This directory houses my written contributions to the field of Data Science and Technology. I use the term **"Essays"** deliberately: whether it is a strictly peer-reviewed paper or an opinionated market analysis, each piece represents an attempt to structure thought, argue a thesis and, most important, share knowledge.

Here, code serves the narrative, not the other way around.

## Structure

* **📂 /Academic**
    * **Focus:** Formal research, methodological rigor and theoretical models.
    * **Content:** Conference papers, journal articles and proceedings.
    
* **📂 /Market**
    * **Focus:** Applied knowledge, industry trends and practical guides.
    * **Content:** Medium articles, tech blog posts and white papers.
    
---
*Note: While the format changes, the goal remains the same: to demystify complexity through clarity.*
